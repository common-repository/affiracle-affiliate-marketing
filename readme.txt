== Installation Guide ==
Follow these simple steps to install and integrate the plugin with your WooCommerce store:

Download and Install: Download the plugin package from our website. In your WordPress dashboard, navigate to "Plugins" and click "Add New." Upload the plugin package and activate it.

Activation: Once activated, navigate to the plugin settings. You'll find an activation prompt where you can enter your Affiracle.com credentials.

Affiracle.com Account: If you haven't registered on Affiracle.com, create an account. Log in to your account and access the "Integration" section.

Plugin Integration: In your Affiracle.com account, follow the provided instructions to complete the integration process. You'll receive a unique API key to connect your WooCommerce store with Affiracle.

Campaign Setup: After integration, configure your campaigns, commission rates, and affiliate tools within your Affiracle.com account.

Start Earning: With everything set up, your WooCommerce store is now ready to benefit from Affiracle's powerful affiliate marketing features. Affiliates can start promoting your products, driving sales, and earning commissions.

Transform your WooCommerce store's marketing strategy with Affiracle's seamless integration. Elevate your affiliate program, expand your reach, and unlock new dimensions of growth. Get started today for a brighter tomorrow in affiliate marketing.
